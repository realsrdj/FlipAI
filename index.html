What is FlipAI?

Homes in the U.S. are selling at a record pace and sale prices are surging to historic highs. Potential homebuyers are finding it more challenging to find the right home at an affordable price and traditional marketplaces like Zillow and Redfin have proven inadequate.

FlipAI is a real estate education and analytics platform that streamlines the single family home-buying experience with predictive analysis powered by the Multiverse™ and crowdsourcing AI.

FlipAI’s machine learning models will use a combination of crowdsourced proprietary data and public data from real estate listings, including photos, spatial data, and textual data, to generate analytics that help users find undervalued homes in their selected markets, from cities to neighborhoods.

How FlipAI Works:

Learn

Learn the fundamentals of real estate investing via a free, no experience required, short educational course.

Complete the course to become certified and be rewarded with FLIP tokens.

Earn

Certified users will be given the opportunity to train FlipAI’s models and continue earning FLIP.

Provide localized real estate data by completing simple tasks to contribute to the crowdsourced AI.

Trusted users with higher stakes in FLIP will have access to tasks that may require more skill with proportionately higher rewards.

Analyze

Users spend FLIP tokens to gain discounted access to unrivaled property intelligence and make informed decisions when purchasing a home listed on the market.
